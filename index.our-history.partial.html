Our History

Fishing in San Diego may not be obvious, but it's worth coming to California. We've had a long way to start our company and offer our customers a wide range of services.

September 2000

Our owner, Steve Marston, became interested in fishing when he bought a ranch in California close to the water.

May 2002

Steve caught the biggest red snapper in his career so far - 13 pounds! This happened by pure chance near Steve's house.

May 2005

Steve started individual guided tours in San Diego, as he wanted more people to try fishing in these great spots.

June 2017

FishExperts was launched. Now we have more than 12 types of guided tours in the San Diego surroundings.
