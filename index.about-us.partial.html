About Us

FishExperts is a dedicated team of skilled guides, fishermen, sailors taking you to the best fishing spots in San Diego.

You can catch fish with your own hands under the guidance of our experienced fishermen. The guides will tell you a lot of interesting stories about the surroundings of San Diego and types of the local fish. You can take the catch home, or our chefs will cook the caught fish on the grill.
